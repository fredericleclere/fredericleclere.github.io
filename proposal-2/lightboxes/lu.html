Tax Information for Luxembourg

Donations are deductible as ‘special expenses’ if the annual total of your donations is €120 or more. The annual deduction is limited to 20% of your total net income, not exceeding the amount of €1M.

For more information, please visit: http://www.impotsdirects.public.lu/az/l/libera_dons/index.html
